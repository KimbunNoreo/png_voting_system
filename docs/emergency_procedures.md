# Emergency Procedures

Emergency procedures exist to preserve election integrity without violating ballot secrecy or zero-trust assumptions.

## Available Controls

- break-glass access with multi-person approval
- global emergency freeze
- device lock and revocation
- manual recount and paper reconciliation
- NID outage fallback under strict controls

## General Principles

- record reason and approvers
- preserve audit evidence before remediation
- prefer containment over silent continuation
- do not disable replay or secrecy controls during emergencies

## Key Playbooks

- token replay attack
- NID unavailable
- time drift detected
- device tamper detected
- election phase violation
